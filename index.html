🛟 Help & Support

📦 How do I buy a product?

Answer: Browse available listings, view product details, and add your preferred item to the cart.

🛒 How can I sell an item?

Answer: Click on "Sell Item", provide the required product information, and submit your listing.

💳 How are payments handled?

Answer: Buyers and sellers can mutually decide the payment method before completing the transaction.

🔒 Is CampusKart safe to use?

Answer: CampusKart is designed for students and promotes secure interactions within the campus community.

🚀 About CampusKart

CampusKart is a student-driven marketplace designed to simplify buying and selling within the campus community. Our platform connects students directly, making it easier to find affordable products, save money, and reduce waste through reuse.

Whether it's textbooks, electronics, furniture, hostel essentials, or daily-use items, CampusKart helps students discover great deals from trusted peers.

✨ Smart. Secure. Student-Friendly.

🎯 Our Mission: To build a trusted campus ecosystem where students can buy, sell, and connect effortlessly.

🌱 Promoting sustainability by encouraging reuse instead of unnecessary purchases.

🤝 Built for Students, by Students.
